Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 21, 2026

FixiHelp ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Fixi mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We collect your name, email address, and phone number when you register for an account to coordinate bookings.
  • Location Data: We collect precise and coarse location data to match you with nearby service professionals and provide navigation.
  • Identifiers: We collect device and user IDs for authentication and security purposes via Firebase.
  • Usage & Diagnostics: We collect crash reports and interaction data to improve app stability and performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information strictly for app functionality, including:

  • Managing your account and delivering requested services.
  • Calculating distances between you and service professionals.
  • Providing customer support and responding to inquiries.
  • Identifying and fixing app crashes.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for cross-context behavioral advertising or tracking across other companies' websites or apps.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request the deletion of your personal information. You can do this by contacting us via email.
